Baltimore Vacants

Browse this map to see vacant properties in Baltimore City.

We use Baltimore City's own open data to get the list of currently vacant properties, and since the data is pulled directly from Baltimore's data feed, it is up to date as soon as the City updates it.

Made in Baltimore by Shea Frederick, with assistance from Jonathan Julian and James Schaffer
